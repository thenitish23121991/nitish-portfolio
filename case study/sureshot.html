Project Brief
Project Brief
The Studycopter Book is aimed at students who are looking for a crash course in exams preparation instead of going through the entire course. It is also devised to be a driver of traffic towards Studycopter online courses and promotion of the Studycopter brand.
Analytics Insights
About the book
The Book consists of short chapters from each of the subjects and topics in the Studycopter online course. The Chapters are a skimmed version of the respective online chapters meant to serve as a crash course for students.
Other Screens
Color Scheme
The Studycopter Color Scheme is designed to be bold and elegant. The colors complement the brand of Studycopter Book.
Other Screens
Typeface
The theme for the Studycopter Book we were looking for was elegant, classic, professional, youthful and fun. We decided to use Frutiger Neue as the sans-serif typeface for the book. We complemented it with Minion Pro as the choice for the serif typeface. It is a modern serif with a large x-height that complements the class and elegance of Frutiger Neue.
Other Screens
Design Elements
The use of text within confinements gives a very sophisticated look to the elements. This design style has been used throughout the design of the book consistently.

Chapters
The Chapters have been designed to be easy to read and interesting and fun at the same time. They enable the student to naturally remember and memorise the important things and takeaways from a chapter. We use color and typographic heirarchy to create a structure in a chapter.
